01 — About

Operations leader with a psychology edge

I hold a BA in Applied Psychology from UNISA — and it's the most useful qualification I have. Understanding how people behave under pressure, how teams fracture, how clients think when their systems are down: that's where I operate differently from most IT operations professionals.

Over 15 years I've built and managed operations across enterprise environments — from first-line support to leading complex implementations, change management, and service delivery at Proudly African, Baseline IT, EOH, Business Connexion, and QContact.

I also build. I founded Phanda AI — a live AI-powered job search and CV tailoring SaaS platform — applying ITIL frameworks to product development. I understand technology from both sides of the delivery chain.

My philosophy: process disciplines execution, psychology disciplines people, and both together produce results that last.
